Eatle

Eatle was a collaboration with two other designers. It explains the meaning behind the claims and labels on grocery store packaging. Eatle aims to educate users by exposing claims that aren't backed by regulations and describing what each verified label actually means.

I primarily worked on the content research, commercial, and social media advertisments.
