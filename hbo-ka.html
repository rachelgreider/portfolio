HBO Key Art

The primary focus of my time working at HBO was key art. I was a primary part of the key art workflow. I resized vertical posters into many different dimensions. These key art posters are used in almost every facet of the HBO brand including: hbo.com, cinemax.com, animated advertisements, and all the major social media sites.

I worked on this summer's season of Hard Knocks throughout the summer. I utilized the key art to create coutdown assets and banners for twitter, facebook, and instagram.

Years and Years is another show that I worked on the key art for. I got to choose the images that went into the show galleries on hbo.com.
