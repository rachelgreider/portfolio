The Mouse and the Moon

The inspiration for this book is a bedtime story my mom told us. It tells the story of a little mouse who goes on a journey to discover if the moon is made out of cheese. My first step was to envision the character and his poses throughout the story. I am working on turning these simple sketches into watercolor sketches. After these initial stages I will create the full layouts in watercolor.
