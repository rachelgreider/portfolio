Lotus

Lotus is a new photography company specializing in portraits. This is a website to showcase the photographer's portfolio. I started out with a simple layout based around rectangles and simple grids. As I thought more about the nature of the photography and the logo I had already created for the brand, I realized my first draft was too stiff for the vibe of Lotus Photography. I integrated the petal shape of the lotus flower throughout the design to make the shapes more organic.
