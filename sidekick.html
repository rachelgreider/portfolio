Sidekick Magazine

Sidekick Magazine is all about the pets in our lives. This first article tackles how pet fish are collected. Juxtaposing commercial fisheries, that collect large quantities of exotic fish and sustain the ornamental fish industry, versus small fisheries that are collect a fraction of the number of fish but contribute to the community's lively hoods.

Sidekick starts off with a contents page and contains three departments with one full length article. One of the departments is Heros. This details the exploits of a heroic figure in the animal community. The title article this month is 'Ornamental Fisheries.' This article contrasts the commercial fisheries that collect tons of fish versus small fisheries that sustain economies. The front cover includes the title article and the page number. The back cover is an advertisement for Sidekick's mobile app.
